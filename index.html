About DREAMRS Lab

The Design Research and Human Factors (DREAMRS) Lab is a research hub dedicated to exploring how designers approach challenges, process information, and innovate. The major focus is design thinking, an intricate process involving cognition, physiology, and neuroscience. This research framework dives into how designers think, make decisions, solve problems, and generate innovative ideas, offering actionable insights to improve design practices, which further impacts human experiences, behaviors, and performance in various contexts, ranging from consumer products to complex systems.

The lab focuses on how designers create solutions that optimize usability, safety, efficiency, feasibility, and overall user satisfaction. These insights fuel innovative approaches to design that cater not only to functional requirements but also to the emotional and cognitive needs of users.

The Design Research and Human Factors Lab is a research hub dedicated to exploring innovative approaches to design and human-centered solutions. Founded and led by Dr. Sonal Keshwani (Ph.D., IISc Bangalore), the lab is associated with the Human Centred Design at IIIT Delhi.

Within DREAMRS Lab

DREAMRS Lab works in the two core areas of design – 'Design Research' and 'Human Factors'

Design Research

We focus on design creativity, methods and tools, biologically inspired design, knowledge based systems, and design processes in resource constrained environments. We aim to improve the ways products, services, and systems are conceptualized and developed, ensuring meaningful advancements in the field of design.

  • • Biologically Inspired Design
  • • Knowledge-Based Systems
  • • Resource-Constrained Design Processes

Human Factors

We study human behavior, capabilities, and limitations to ensure that our designs align with user needs. By integrating product and cognitive ergonomics, our research addresses critical aspects of safety, performance, and well-being, delivering optimized systems and products for real-world applications.

  • • Product Ergonomics
  • • Safety and Performance Optimization
  • • User-Centered Design

Courses Offered

The DREAMRS Lab offers a range of courses designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in design research and human factors. Our courses blend theoretical foundations with practical applications, preparing students to tackle real-world challenges in usability, ergonomics, and innovation

DES102

Introduction to Human Computer Interaction

This course covers HCI principles, focusing on usability and prototyping through hands-on projects

DES524

Ergonomics/ Human Factors for Design

This course explores ergonomics in design, emphasizing human factors, usability, and product evaluation

DES523

Cognition & Information Processing in Design

This course examines human cognition in design, focusing on perception, memory, and mental workload assessment

DES533

Interaction Design Perspectives & Methods

This course covers design methods, user analysis, data evaluation, prototyping, and usability testing
